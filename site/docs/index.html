Architecture

Keplor is structured as a Rust workspace with five crates:

CratePurpose
keplor-coreEvent, Provider, Usage, Cost types and error enums
keplor-serverHTTP server, ingestion pipeline, route handlers
keplor-storeSQLite storage, content-addressed dedup, zstd compression
keplor-pricingLiteLLM pricing catalog loader and cost computation
keplor-cliThe keplor binary with all commands

Design principles

PrincipleWhat it means
Pure observationalNever reformat, rewrite, or modify payloads
Heavy compressionzstd with trained dictionaries, content-addressed dedup
Zero-dep defaultSQLite works out of the box, no external services required
Lean binarySingle static musl binary under 10 MB
